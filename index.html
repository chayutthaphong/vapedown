Disclaimer & References

Disclaimer: This application provides a structural framework for nicotine tapering and is intended for personal data collection and longitudinal tracking. It does not constitute formal medical advice, diagnosis, or behavioral therapy. Please consult healthcare professionals for personalized guidance.

Clinical Rationale: The protocol operates on the premise that pre-filled closed-system devices cannot be diluted. Dependence reduction relies strictly on modifying behavioral frequency, interval extension, and trigger decoupling based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) principles.

References:

  • Truth Initiative. (2021). Actionable strategies for e-cigarette cessation: Tapering and behavioral modification.
