Disclaimer & References

Disclaimer: This application provides a structural framework for nicotine tapering and is intended for personal tracking. It does not constitute formal medical advice or behavioral therapy.

Protocol Basis: The 8-week step-down schedule is modeled for closed-system 3% (30mg/mL) electronic nicotine delivery systems (ENDS). Because dilution is impossible, the protocol utilizes interval extension, puff limitation, and trigger decoupling (Cognitive Behavioral principles) to systematically reduce plasma nicotine concentration and manage withdrawal symptoms.
